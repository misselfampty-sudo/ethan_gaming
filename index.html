💡 Game Architecture (Behind the Scenes)

  • Dashboard Navigation: We use simple CSS display: none and display: block toggles in JavaScript to switch screens without reloading the webpage!
  • The Game Loop: Pressing "Back to Arcade" stops the requestAnimationFrame loop completely. This saves battery power by letting your computer stop calculating when you aren't playing!
  • Controls: Use the ◀ Left Arrow and ▶ Right Arrow keys to slide your ship back and forth.
